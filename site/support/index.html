Old Device Clock & Calendar

Old Device Clock & Calendar is a simple offline clock and monthly calendar app for older iPhone and iPad devices.

What the app does

  • Displays an analog clock and monthly calendar
  • Works in light mode and dark mode
  • Lets you change the calendar week start day
  • Runs without sign-in, ads, or online setup

Support contact

Compatibility

Designed for older iPhone and iPad devices and intended to work as an offline clock and calendar display.
